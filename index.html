About Sip&Grow

Sip&Grow is on a mission to reduce single-use waste with a cup that’s durable, repairable, and designed for the circular economy. From materials to lifecycle, every detail is engineered for longevity and minimal footprint.

Sustainability First

  • Responsible materials chosen for durability and recyclability.
  • Modular design so parts can be replaced instead of discarded.
  • End-of-life take-back program planned for true circularity.
